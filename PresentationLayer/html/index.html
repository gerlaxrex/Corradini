Welcome to Centro Corradini

Intro

The psychological wellness of human beings finds its own roots in the capability of the armonious feeling of each's personal exeprience.
In the confluence between the human and the nature it is possible to discover or re-discover the possibility of a full life, sense-wide open, and the experience of living in the entire world.

The Garden Cure

So the so called "Garden Cure" constantly assumes increasing importance in different fields of study; in particular as a therapy for the reabilitation of people affected by psycho-physical diseases.
The Garden Cure affects a deeper and more intimate aspect of the human psyche, being able to reactivate not only some bonds with the nature that nowadays have been lost, but also giving to the person a sense of responsibilty and dynamicity that maybe isn't vivid anymore.

Our center is then fully focused on the use of the Garden Cure in order to help people with Autism Spectrum Disorder of various ages not only by psychological sustain coming from the experts associated with the association, but also teaching them how to effectively work on a project as a squad and orienting them to the work life, whereas possible.
