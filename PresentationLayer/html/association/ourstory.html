The Association

Our Story

The Association has been founded in 2016 by Luciano Rotolo, the Association President, and by other actual associates due to the lack of a principal and figure of reference for people affected by the autism spectrum disease.

The project led by the association is aimed to young adult people with High Functioning Autism (HFA) disease. Various activities are orgranized in order to strenghten the personal autonomy and community integration of the subjects: implant and cure of a bionatural garden as a Garden Therapy function.

The second main purpose for the association in its years of activity has been the introduction of the people with disabilities in the labour market, through the activities done in the gardens and in the shops.
This is fundamental due to the necessities of the families to have an answer to their constant and heavy question: "What after we are gone?".
So, through the formation and direct practical experience it is possible to consolidate the base for a future project of cooperation among the participants, also with the mediation of a legal entity that will make them possible to start a real job activity for the associates.
