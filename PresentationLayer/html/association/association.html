The Association

Introduction

The Centro Corradini Association is involved in the sustain of young people affected by autism spectre disease. Activities of various nature are organized in order to support the boys and let them comfortable for the insertion on the work.
The people working with us cover different roles and are involved in various type of activities! If you want to become an associate by participating to some activities or simply by supporting us by making a donation, don't hesitate to contact us! Info about how to reach us can be found in the section "Where we are" or in the "Contacts" section. If you want to know more about our activities and the services we offer for our associates explore the "Services" section!
